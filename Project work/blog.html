  • Post by Tanaya Verma
  • |
  • 30 March 2021

I’m often asked this question, how to make granola if I don’t have an oven. The logical answer would be to dry roast all the grains, nuts and seeds until crisp in a large heavy-bottomed pan. Warm up the honey mixed with the flavourings, toss all the ingredients in this and allow to set in a wide tray. Once the homemade granola has cooled, crumble and store in an airtight container.
